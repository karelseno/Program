Vaccination Overview

Vaccinations are a vital part of your child's health care, protecting them from serious and potentially life-threatening diseases. Our pediatric center follows the recommended immunization schedule to ensure your child stays safe and healthy.

  • Hepatitis B (HepB): Protects against liver infection.
  • Diphtheria, Tetanus, and Pertussis (DTaP): Prevents whooping cough, tetanus, and diphtheria.
  • Polio (IPV): Protects against poliovirus.
  • Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib): Prevents meningitis and pneumonia.
  • Pneumococcal Conjugate (PCV13): Protects against pneumococcal diseases.
  • Rotavirus (RV): Prevents severe diarrhea.
  • Measles, Mumps, and Rubella (MMR): Protects against measles, mumps, and rubella.
  • Varicella (Chickenpox): Prevents chickenpox.
  • Influenza (Flu): Annual vaccine to protect against seasonal flu.

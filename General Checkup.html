General Checkups Overview

Regular pediatric checkups are essential to monitor your child's growth, development, and overall health. Our comprehensive checkups help detect any potential health issues early and provide guidance for a healthy lifestyle.

  • Growth and Development Assessment
  • Immunization Updates
  • Vision and Hearing Screening
  • Nutrition and Dietary Counseling
  • Behavioral and Emotional Health Evaluation
  • Physical Examination
  • Dental Health Check
  • Safety and Injury Prevention Guidance
